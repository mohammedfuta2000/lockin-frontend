FAQ

How does lockin work?

Create goals with deadlines. Connect social media. AI generates posts. At deadline, posts go live automatically.

Can I delete a goal?

No. That's the point. Goals can only be completed or postponed (max 2 hours).

What if I miss my deadline?

Posts go live automatically. That's the accountability mechanism.

Can I edit posts before they go live?

Yes. You get a notification 2 hours before deadline with the option to edit.

Which social media platforms are supported?

Currently: Twitter, LinkedIn, and Reddit.

How do I disconnect a social account?

Settings → Connected Accounts → Tap platform → Disconnect
