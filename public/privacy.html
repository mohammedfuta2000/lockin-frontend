Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

What We Collect

  • Email address (for authentication via Supabase)
  • Social media usernames (Twitter, LinkedIn, Reddit)
  • Goal titles and descriptions
  • Push notification tokens

How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate users via Supabase Auth
  • Generate and post goals to connected social media
  • Send deadline reminders via push notifications
  • Improve app functionality

Data Storage

All data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL database). OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting
  • OpenAI: AI-powered post generation
  • Firebase: Push notifications
  • Twitter API: Post to Twitter
  • LinkedIn API: Post to LinkedIn

Your Rights

You can delete your account at any time through the app settings. Upon deletion, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

To request a copy of your data, contact us at privacy@lockin.cloud

Children's Privacy

lockin is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes via email or in-app notification.
