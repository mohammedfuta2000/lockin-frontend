Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Sign Out → Contact support@lockin.cloud to request account deletion. All your data will be permanently removed.

Can I cancel a goal after creating it?

No. That's the point of lockin. Goals can only be completed or postponed (max 2 hours). Once you create a goal, you're committed.

What happens if I miss my deadline?

Your goal will be automatically posted to all selected social media platforms. This is the accountability mechanism - there's no backing out.
